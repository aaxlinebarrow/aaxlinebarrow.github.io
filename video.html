Video: Dan's Bandana Project

Dan's Bandana Project is a program that seeks to bring awareness to the prevalence of mental health, especially on college campuses. We want to remove the stigmatism surrounding mental health issues by showing support through wearing a white bandana on our backpacks or on our person. Participants of this project are signaling their support for the project and also serving as a resource or friend to others that may need it. We have access to hotline numbers if you don't, and we are always willing to sit and listen if that's what you need.
